Statistics

Statistics is our way of describing properties of a large group using only some of its members. This is useful in surveys and experiments where you can't get acess to every member of a group.

In this interactive article, we will explain and visualize these concepts using data that you choose, at the moment it is full of random numbers.

Mean and Standard Deviation

The mean is another word for "average". The average of the data can be thought of as the "average sample" that you'd expect if you picked one on average. For this reason it is also called the expected value.
